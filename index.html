About Me

Brgy Puspus

Puspus is a barangay in the city of San Fernando, in the province of La Union. It is part of the Ilocos Region (Region I) on the island of Luzon. The population as determined by the 2020 Census was 578. This represented 0.46% of the total population of San Fernando.
